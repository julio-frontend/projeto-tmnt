"Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles" (TMNT) is a multimedia franchise that follows the exploits of four anthropomorphic turtles—Leonardo, Michelangelo, Donatello, and Raphael—who, trained in ninjutsu by their rat sensei Splinter, fight crime in New York City. Originating as a comic book series in 1984 by Kevin Eastman and Peter Laird, TMNT has expanded into animated TV shows, movies, video games, and more. Known for its action-packed adventures and themes of camaraderie, TMNT has captivated audiences worldwide, becoming a cultural icon with its unique blend of humor, martial arts action, and memorable characters.
